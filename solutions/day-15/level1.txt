#Q 01] What is a package?
  - a collection of predefined methods to solve specific problem set

#Q 02] What is a third party package?
  - a package that was not primarily developed by the same creators of a specific platform for it's intended for. 

#Q 03] Do you have to use third party packages?
  - yes, most probably

#Q 04] How do you know the popularity and stability of a third-party package?
  - considering the number of downloads, community engagement, usages, version history & documentation quality

#Q 05] How many JavaScript packages are there on the npm registry?
  - over 1.4 million

#Q 06] How do you install a third party package?
  - using the terminal through NODE-PACAKAGE-MANAGER or YARN
